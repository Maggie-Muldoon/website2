Wedding Art

Your wedding day is one of the most beautiful days of your life — Maggie is there to paint it as it happens. She captures the emotion, the light, the love, and the people in a sweeping live painting that becomes an heirloom you'll cherish forever.

Unlike a photograph, a live wedding painting carries the warmth and feeling of the moment — painted by hand with love, in real time. It's a truly unique experience for you and your guests, and a one-of-a-kind piece to hang in your home.

What's included

  • Live painting of your ceremony, reception, or a special moment of your choice
  • Large-format piece (typically 24×36" or custom size) — all materials included
  • Pre-event consultation to plan the composition and style
  • Display as a guest attraction during your reception
  • Final finishing touches after the event — delivered to your door
