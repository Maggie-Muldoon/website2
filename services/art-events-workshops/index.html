Art Events & Workshops

Why watch art being made when you can make it yourself? Maggie leads hands-on painting sessions where your guests pick up a brush and create their own artwork — with her guidance every step of the way. No experience needed.

These workshops are endlessly popular for team-building events, bachelorette parties, birthday celebrations, and community gatherings. Everyone leaves with a piece they're genuinely proud of — and great memories of creating it.

What's included

  • Guided group painting session led by Maggie — no experience necessary
  • All supplies provided — canvases, paints, brushes, and more
  • Custom themes available to match your event's vibe or brand
  • Guests take their finished artwork home
  • Great for groups of 10–50+ people; corporate and private bookings welcome
